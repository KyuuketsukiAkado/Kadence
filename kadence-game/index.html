About us

Welcome to the Kadence Garage!

What started as a solo attempt to "fix terrible bike-shop websites" quickly shifted gears into a full-blown indie game. We are a small, passion-driven team of three, trying to build a cozy simulator where empathy and bike geometry matter more than carbon fiber and marketing.

Thank you for playing our demo. Please don't put 160mm rotors on a heavy E-bike. Your knees will thank you.

The Crew:

Hanna Muzyka aka repro4chful — Art Director, UX/UI & Product Owner (Drew every single pixel and dog you see here).

LemiTZ — Co-Scriptwriter, Lead English Localizator (The voice behind the quirky customers and Ash's sarcasm).
